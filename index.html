COPPA

The internet is a extremely useful tool and resource for information to those of all ages. However, it is so widespread and difficult to monitor that parents are having a hard time protecting one of the most easily influenced age groups, young children. Not only does the internet act as a tool for finding useful information, but it can also be used as a tool for communication between a wide range of online users. This enables users to communicate anonymously making it very difficult for you to really know who you are talking to.
